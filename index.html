Self-Perception in the era of cyborgs

For final final project in my bachelor's degree, I created an interactive installation that explores how humans see themselves in the age of technological progress. The participants will be faced with a mirror in which they see not only their reflection but also an interactive projection which reacts to their movements. The projection will initially contour their bodies but as time goes on, it will morph into different shapes, becoming more chaotic, representing how external factors change our own view of who we are.
